Welcome!

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Economics at Texas Tech University. I also supervise our dedicated team of graduate instructors and teaching assistants. I lead a weekly workshop aimed at enhancing their teaching techniques and effectiveness. I am also an adjunct instructor at the Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center, where I teach health economics courses. My research interests include computational economics and healthcare economics.

Before joining Texas Tech, I was an Assistant Professor of Economics at South Plains College for eleven years. Prior to joining higher-ed, I was an investment and business banker. I'm passionate about teaching economics and am excited to be a part of such an innovative and research-focused department. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions!
